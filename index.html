We understand that a spatial expression is very important and sensitive aspect of a day to day life. Wherein a better design may change the energy levels of the inhabitants, a sub-standard design and execution may result into a total loss. For that matter, providing better habitable spaces are always our priority. We promise the best of our services with all the clarities regarding the project.

New Ideas Design

Residential and Commercial Spaces:
The Floating Gardens Skyscraper would house a mix of residential apartments and commercial spaces. The residential units would offer residents access to the lush green surroundings, while the commercial areas could include offices, shops, and communal facilities.

New Ideas Design

Vertical Farms:
The skyscraper incorporates multiple levels of vertical farms, utilizing hydroponic or aeroponic systems to grow a variety of crops. These farms would provide a sustainable source of fresh produce for the building's occupants and potentially even surrounding communities.

New Ideas Design

Hanging Gardens:
The building's exterior features a series of vertical gardens that cascade down the façade, creating a visually striking green oasis in the urban landscape. These gardens would not only enhance the aesthetics of the structure but also improve air quality and provide natural insulation.
